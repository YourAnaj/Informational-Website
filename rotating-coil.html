Rotating Coil in a Magnetic Field

Example Problem:

A coil with 100 turns and an area of 0.06 m² rotates in a uniform magnetic field of 0.4 T at 75 Hz. What is the peak EMF generated?

Solution:

Use the induced EMF formula for a rotating coil: EMFpeak = NBAω

Where:

  • N = number of turns (100)
  • B = magnetic field (0.4 T)
  • A = coil area (0.06 m²)
  • ω = angular velocity (2π*f)
